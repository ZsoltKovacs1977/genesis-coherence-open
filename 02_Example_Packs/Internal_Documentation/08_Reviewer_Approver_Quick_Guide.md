# Reviewer / Approver Quick Guide

## Review principle
AI may support drafting. Humans remain responsible for release, approval, and operational use.

## Review checks
Before approval, confirm:
- the task is clear
- the source material was allowed
- the output is usable
- any important claims were checked
- the tone and format are fit for purpose
- no restricted or sensitive content has been mishandled

## Escalate when
- ownership is unclear
- the reviewer cannot verify the output
- the task may shape a formal decision
- restricted or confidential data may have been used improperly
- the content could cause material operational risk if wrong
